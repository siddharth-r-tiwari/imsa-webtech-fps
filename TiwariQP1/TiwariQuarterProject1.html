About Us:

Welcome to IMSA Car Rental! Because students don't have the opportunity to park their own cars on campus, IMSA loans out cars for a low price! Renting from IMSA will leave you satisified as well as serve for it's greater good. A portion of the money goes to the IMSA fund which will be used for other projects, for you! You can rent an economy vehicle for $29.99 a day and a luxury vehicle for $49.99 a day! Click on the model names to see information about each vehicle. If a car is not rented already, you can preselect them in the information boxes (If you open the form it will already be preselected)! Safe driving!
